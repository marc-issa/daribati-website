How Daribati Works

Daribati simplifies Lebanese tax calculations so you can focus on what matters. Whether you are an employee estimating your salary deductions, a property owner computing built property tax, or a business professional filing deemed profit — our calculators handle the complexity for you.

1

Choose a Calculator

Select from salary tax, property tax, deemed profit, real estate fees, NSSF contributions, or taxi driver rates. Each calculator follows current Lebanese fiscal regulations.

2

Enter Your Details

Provide the required information such as income amounts, property values, family status, or profession category. The app supports both Lebanese Pound and US Dollar amounts.

3

Get Instant Results

Receive a detailed breakdown of your tax obligations or fee estimates instantly. All calculations are based on the latest Lebanese tax rates, brackets, and regulations.
